Clinical proteomics summary for a translational human health cohort.
Samples were collected from plasma together with patient metadata.
The analysis focused on biomarkers linked to inflammation and tissue repair.
Quality control removed spectra with insufficient peptide support.
Searching for robust biomarkers requires reproducible signal across batches.
Sequence variants were reviewed for possible effects on protein stability.
Structural annotations were integrated with disease-associated pathways.
